Level 1 
Your partner, Jake, is missing! He was investigating the weapons manufacturer Nexkon when he went radio silent. Your Commissioner reacted suspiciously when you called it in, and asked you to return and file a full report. You have a different plan. Let’s rescue Jake!  Use WASD to navigate through the entrance, the yellow teleporter will take you further into the Nexkon facility. Make sure you don't run out of fuel.
